## THE THREE PRODUCT BETS

### Bet 1: Pedimento Confidence Score
A number from 0-100 that tells Tito exactly how much he needs to review. At 96%, he glances and approves. At 72%, he reads every field. The score is calibrated against his actual approval history — not a guess.

**Why it matters:** This is what enables autonomous transmission. When confidence > 95% AND the supplier has 10+ prior clearances AND all 9 verification gates pass — CRUZ transmits without asking.

### Bet 2: Supplier Intelligence Graph
A living database of every supplier relationship across all CRUZ clients. Each supplier has a profile: fracción history, value patterns, document compliance rate, crossing times, common issues.

**Why it matters:** Onboarding client #10 takes 10 minutes instead of 10 days because CRUZ already knows their suppliers. The graph compounds with every shipment. It never degrades. It only gets richer.

### Bet 3: Crossing Predictor
Real data on which days, which bridges, which times produce the fastest clearance. Not guesses — measured from thousands of actual crossings at Aduana 240.

**Why it matters:** This is what logistics directors switch brokers for. "Your Thursday shipments clear in 3.1 days on average. If you shifted to Wednesday, it would be 2.4 days. Here's why." Nobody else can say this because nobody else has the data.
